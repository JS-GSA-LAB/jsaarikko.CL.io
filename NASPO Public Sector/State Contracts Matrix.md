## Extreme Networks Advantage

With 3 nationwide cooperative contracts (NASPO ValuePoint, PEPPM, Sourcewell) plus 4 regional programs (OETC, MHEC, COSTARS, MICTA), every US state has at least 3 procurement paths. Top states have 5 contracts available. This extensive coverage simplifies public sector procurement and accelerates time-to-purchase compared to vendors with limited cooperative presence.
