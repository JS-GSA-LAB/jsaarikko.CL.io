# Ubiquiti

> **Disruptive price-performance**
> Flagship: UniFi, UISP

## Market Position

| Category | Quadrant | Note |
|----------|----------|------|
| Wireless | Niche Player | Price disruptor, limited enterprise support |
| Switching | Niche Player | Price leader, basic L2/L3 |
| Management | Niche Player | Free but no enterprise management features |

## Strengths & Weaknesses

### Wireless
**Strengths:**
- Lowest cost
- No licensing fees
- Active community
- Simple deployment

**Weaknesses:**
- No enterprise support/SLA
- No NAC or advanced security
- Limited scalability
- No dedicated sales team

### Switching
**Strengths:**
- Lowest price point
- Free management
- Good for SOHO/SMB

**Weaknesses:**
- No fabric or segmentation
- No enterprise support
- Basic features only

### Management
**Strengths:**
- Completely free
- Self-hosted option
- Simple UI
- No recurring cost

**Weaknesses:**
- No enterprise features
- No SLA
- No AIOps
- Community support only

## Licensing

- **Model:** Free (No Licensing)
- **Mandatory:** No
- **Free Tier:** Yes
- **Hardware Works Without License:** Yes
- **Tiers:** Free — no license tiers
- **Lock-In:** Low — No licensing lock-in whatsoever. But proprietary hardware and limited standards support create soft lock-in.
- **Exit Cost:** Low — Low-cost hardware means low stranded asset cost. But no enterprise migration tools or professional services.

## Hidden Costs

| Cost Category | Amount | Risk |
|---------------|--------|------|
| Training & Certification | $0 | LOW |
| Professional Services | $0-$5,000 | LOW |
| Migration Complexity | Low | LOW |
| Multi-Platform Tax | None | LOW |
| License True-Up Surprises | None | LOW |
| Vendor Lock-In Risk | Low | LOW |

- **Training & Certification** ($0): No formal training. Community resources only. Simple platform.
- **Professional Services** ($0-$5,000): No vendor PS. Independent installers. Simple deployments.
- **Migration Complexity** (Low): Low-cost hardware. Easy to rip and replace due to low sunk cost.
- **Multi-Platform Tax** (None): Unified UniFi ecosystem. But missing enterprise features forces third-party additions.
- **License True-Up Surprises** (None): No licenses = no surprises. But also no vendor support when things break.
- **Vendor Lock-In Risk** (Low): Low cost = low switching cost. But no enterprise migration tools or support.
