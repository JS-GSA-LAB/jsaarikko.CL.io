## Dimension Descriptions

- **Feature Completeness:** Breadth and depth of networking features across wireless, switching, SD-WAN, and management
- **Cloud & AI Innovation:** Cloud-native architecture maturity, AIOps capabilities, digital twin, and AI assistant quality
- **Licensing Flexibility:** Availability of perpetual, subscription, consumption, and free-tier options; lock-in risk
- **Deployment Simplicity:** Ease of initial deployment, zero-touch provisioning, and day-2 operations complexity
- **Support Quality:** TAC responsiveness, SLA options, documentation quality, and professional services
- **Ecosystem & Integration:** Partner ecosystem breadth, API coverage, third-party integrations, and automation tooling
- **Built-in Security:** Native security features: NAC, segmentation, ZTNA, encryption, and threat detection
- **Scalability:** Ability to scale from SMB to large enterprise and multi-site deployments
